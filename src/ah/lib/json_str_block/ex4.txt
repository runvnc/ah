# Simple Chicken Casserole Recipe

## Ingredients:
- 4 chicken breasts, diced
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 package of stuffing mix
- 1/2 cup butter, melted

## Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Spread diced chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
3. Mix soup and sour cream, pour over chicken.
4. Sprinkle cheese on top.
5. Combine stuffing mix with melted butter, spread over cheese.
6. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden and bubbly.

Enjoy your homemade chicken casserole!
